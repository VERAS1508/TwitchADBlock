# Privacy Policy — Twitch AdBlock

**Last updated:** June 2025

## Summary

Twitch AdBlock does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer or any third party.

## Data stored locally

The extension stores one setting on your device using `chrome.storage.local`:

| Key | Purpose | Default |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `enabled` | Whether ad blocking is turned on | `true` |

This preference stays on your device. It is not synced, uploaded, or shared.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why it is needed |
| --- | --- |
| `storage` | Persist the on/off toggle |
| `tabs` | Reload Twitch tabs after you change the setting |
| Host access (`twitch.tv`, `ttvnw.net`, `gql.twitch.tv`) | Run the content script on Twitch pages |

## Third parties

The extension does not communicate with any backend operated by this project. When you use Twitch, Twitch's own privacy policy applies to your use of their service.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue in the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/VERAS1508/TwitchADBlock).
